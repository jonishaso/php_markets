About Us

Ruizean Markets is an ASIC regulated brokerage providing sophisticated and wholesale investors top tier technology coupled with deep interbank liquidity. We provide an array of products through Foreign Exchange, Metals, Commodities and Indices.

Our Mission Statement

At Ruizean, our mission is to become a world leader in our space providing our clients with the highest level of service, lowest spreads and greatest execution speeds available in the market.

Our Core Values

Professional and reliable: We believe that our customers deserve a professional and reliable service. At Ruizean, the client always comes first. This philosophy is essential to our values and ensures that our staff will endeavour to offer you the premium experience possible.

 

Honesty and integrity: Integrity and honest behaviour is the hallmark of any great organisation. Over the past few years Ruizean Markets has built a formidable reputation for both.

 

Superior customer service: As a client of Ruizean Markets you will be treated like a VIP. We are dedicated to answering all your questions and helping you maximise your potential as a trader.

 

Subscribe to innovation:Our team are focussed on bringing you innovations to compliment some of the best and latest in technology and pricing. We are continually looking to help you achieve your trading goals and offer a first-class trading experience.
