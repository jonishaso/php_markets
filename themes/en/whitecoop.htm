White Label Partner

If you provide financial services to your customers, then if you become a white label partner you can offer foreign exchange products to your customers and further expand your business.

On the other hand, you might want to enter the forex broker industry yourself as a trader, and in this case, being a white label partner simplifies and facilitates the growth of your new business.

A white label partnership with Ruizean Markets is a business solution that will let you use part of your forex trading and CFD online trading platform to set up your own business. Using our top-ranked MT 4 trading platform, your customers will have access to a wide range of products and markets at their fingertips.

When you participate in the Ruizean Markets white label program, you can take advantage of our deep liquidity, fast execution speeds and cutting-edge technology. You can easily integrate your own business model with our online trading platform, and with your own personal panel, the management of your business.

Benefits of a White Label Partner:

  • You can offer a wide range of products in the global market to your customers.
  • We have experienced professionals to guide you every step of the way.
  • You can set up your own business offering foreign exchange trading solutions.
  • Easy-to-configure software to meet your business needs.
  • You can sign into your administrative panel, anytime, anywhere to manage your customer accounts.
