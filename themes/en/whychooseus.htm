Why Choose Us

Our Brokerage Transactions are supervised by the Australian Securities and Investments Commission (ASIC). We adhere to the world's highest standards of corporate governance, enhancing our reputation and credibility.

We offer the world's most popular trading software MT4. We are focussed on providing our clients with the necessary tools to enable access to an unrivalled trading environment.

In times of volatility and thin liquidity, we have a range of liquidity providers, securing reliable and stable conditions for our traders, and ensuring that all orders are consistently executed. Our deep liquidity also allows us to provide better pricing structures and flawless executions for large positions.

We offer a wealth of different trading products to our wholesale clients, including pricing on over 65 different currency pairs, commodities such as gold, silver and oil and also access to some of the major indices of the world including Nasdaq, the S&P 500, the Dow Jones 30, FTSE 100, DAX30, and Hong Kong's Hang Seng 50.

We offer a variety of safe and convenient payment methods, including wire transfers, China Union Pay (CUP), Skrill and Neteller.

Ruizean Markets is committed to providing unparalleled customer service and assistance. Our support team have a sound financial background in the FX industry therefore they understand the needs of our clients. We base our growth on putting the customer first so you know you can rely on Ruizean Markets, 24/5.
