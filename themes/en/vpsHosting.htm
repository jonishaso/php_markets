VPS Hosting
What Is VPS Hosting?

Essentially, VPS hosting is like having a dedicated server in a shared hosting environment. With a VPS you have the affordability of shared hosting, but the power, control and speed of a dedicated server, along with dedicated resources. You can access your VPS via any computer or mobile device removing the requirement to rely on one single computer to access your trading accounts.


A VPS allows you to run automated forex trading strategies, including EA, 24 hours a day, seven days a week. Your VPS will continue to execute your automated trades even in the unlikely event of a power outage. A VPS is ideal for customers who rely on automated trading when factoring in slippage for customers who prefer to perform manual trades.


Why Use VPS Hosting?

If you are a professional forex trader and use the MetaTrader 4 trading platform (24 hours a day online), then a VPS (Virtual Dedicated Server) is the ideal choice. A VPS lets you trade anywhere in the world on any computer, offers robust security, fast speeds, reduces slippage and even operates in power outages. Many forex traders use a VPS, as it provides an uninterrupted operating environment.
